About Us

For over 25 years, Harnoor Pallak Construction has been crafting exceptional homes, commercial spaces, and landmark renovations across Calgary and Alberta. We build with precision, passion, and an unwavering commitment to excellence.

Building Legacies.
One Masterpiece at a Time.

Since 2001, Harnoor Pallak Construction has been Calgary’s most trusted name in premium residential and commercial building. We don’t just construct buildings — we create enduring spaces that reflect our clients’ vision and stand the test of time.

From luxury custom homes in the foothills to sophisticated commercial developments downtown, every project we undertake is guided by meticulous craftsmanship, superior materials, and a deep respect for the architectural heritage of Alberta.
